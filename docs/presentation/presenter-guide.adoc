== Common Audience Questions

=== "Is this just another model server?"

[quote]
No. fleet-llm-d doesn't serve models — it orchestrates the fleet of model servers. OVMS C++ serves the models. fleet-llm-d manages where they run, how traffic routes, when to scale, who gets what quota, and records every decision.

=== "Why not just use HPA?"

[quote]
HPA is reactive — it scales after the spike hits. fleet-llm-d's predictive brain scales before the spike. Event profiles pre-warm for known events. SLO forecasting catches trends before they breach. HPA is still there as the safety net.

=== "What if the predictive brain is wrong?"

[quote]
The policy evaluator is the safety gate. Confidence below threshold? Deferred. Too many replicas? Refused. Critical action? Requires human approval. And every prediction-action-outcome chain is in the ledger, so you can measure accuracy over time.

=== "Does this work with GPUs too?"

[quote]
Yes. fleet-llm-d manages GPU clusters, CPU clusters, or mixed fleets. The cost engine calculates chargeback across H200, B200, H100, A100, MI300X, L40, and CPU. CPU inference is the cost optimization story — not the only story.

=== "What about multi-cluster?"

[quote]
That's fleet-llm-d's core design. The Rust gateway routes traffic across clusters. PlacementPolicy spreads models geographically. KVCacheTransferPolicy migrates hot caches between clusters. The fleet controller federates metrics across all clusters.
